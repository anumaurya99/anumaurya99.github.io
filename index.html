— What is WeChitra

Oddly Beautiful Vision

Wechitra houses artists, where they are given freedom to perform. We believe that Art that is constructed by a free soul is always true to its core. From personal projects and documentaries to big-budget productions, we sell it all. We listen, and we communicate. Wechitra is a bridge between client and content, connecting you to the vast universe of creation. You point, we shoot. Wechitra is here to live a hundred stories that are brewing within you, be a part of your stories, and invite you to be a part of ours.

— How we work

The Process

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We immerse ourselves in your world — understanding your story, audience, and the feeling you want to leave behind.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Visual treatment, shot lists, mood boards, and storyboards. We design the film before picking up a camera.

  3. 03

    Production

    On set, on location — precision execution by our experienced crew with professional-grade cinema equipment.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    Edit, grade, sound design, and final delivery in every format. Revision rounds until it's exactly right.
