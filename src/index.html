— Who we are

Oddly
Beautiful
Vision

Wechitra houses artists where they are given freedom to perform. We believe that Art that is constructed by a free soul always sells. From intimate projects and documentaries to big budget productions, we sell it all. We listen and we communicate. Not a mechanical system that chew your words and spit out an output. We are a breathing system that grows and the people here want to give their best to the art of creation. Wechitra is a bridge between client and content, connecting you to the vast universe of creation. You point, we shoot. Wechitra is here to live a hundred stories that are brewing within you, be a part of your stories and invite you to be a part of ours.
